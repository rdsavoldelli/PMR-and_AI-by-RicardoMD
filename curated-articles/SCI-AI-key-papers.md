## 4. Molecular Therapeutics and Regenerative Medicine

The intersection where AI discovers biological treatments.

*   **AI and Robotics in Stabilizing the ChABC Enzyme**
    *   **Summary:** Researchers used AI and robotics to stabilize the enzyme *Chondroitinase ABC* (ChABC). This enzyme can degrade scar tissue in the spinal cord (which inhibits nerve regeneration) but is unstable at body temperature. AI identified copolymers capable of keeping the enzyme active, potentially promoting tissue regeneration.

## 5. Evaluation of LLMs (ChatGPT/Gemini) in Clinical Practice

Comparative studies on the reliability of Generative AI as a clinical decision support tool.

*   **Comparative Analysis of ChatGPT-4o and Google Gemini Against ACS Guidelines**
    *   **Summary:** A study evaluating the concordance of these models with the 2022 American College of Surgeons (ACS) guidelines.
        *   **Results:** ChatGPT-4o showed 73% concordance; Gemini 69%.
        *   **Limitations:** Both models hallucinated (e.g., suggesting steroids for gunshot wounds to the spine, which is contraindicated) and relied on outdated protocols regarding tracheostomy timing.
    *   **Conclusion:** Useful as supplementary tools but currently unsafe for autonomous clinical decisions in trauma.
    *   **Tags:** `LLM`, `ChatGPT`, `Gemini`, `Clinical Guidelines`, `ACS`.
    *   **Citation (ABNT):**
        > YU, Alexander et al. Evaluating Artificial Intelligence in Spinal Cord Injury Management: A Comparative Analysis of ChatGPT-4o and Google Gemini Against American College of Surgeons Best Practices Guidelines for Spine Injury. **Global Spine Journal**, v. 15, n. 7, p. 3199‚Äì3220, 2025.
